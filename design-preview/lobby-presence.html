Who's already here

A live read of how full the room is before you commit to joining — shown on the pre-join lobby, updating gently as people come and go while you set up your camera and name. Three directions, each a different answer to the one real question: how much do we reveal to someone who hasn't joined yet? Empty rooms lean into the brand — you'll make the first ripple.

Reveals: who is in the roomРаскрывает: кто в комнате
My recommendation — ship B (count + faceless circles). It gives the human warmth of “there are real people in here” and a glanceable sense of the room's size, while revealing no identity. It's the honest middle: friendlier than a bare number, safe by default.
The tradeoff to flag — names before joining (C) is a probe risk. Anyone holding a room link could open the lobby and watch who is inside without ever joining or being seen. That's fine for internal / known-team rooms, so keep C as an opt-in the host enables; never the default for public or link-shared rooms. A stays as the ultra-safe fallback (e.g. 1:1s or privacy-sensitive tenants). Same component, one reveal prop — 'count' | 'faceless' | 'roster'.

04

The gentle update

People join & leave while you're still deciding. It should never jump.
Live — runningЖивое — идёт B
3 in the callв звонке

Auto-plays. Or nudge it: a new circle ripples in and the number rolls up; on leave the stack closes the gap and rolls down. Motion off / reduced-motion → the value just swaps, no animation.Идёт сам. Или подтолкните: новый кружок появляется рябью, число прокручивается вверх; при выходе стопка смыкается. Motion off / reduced-motion → значение меняется без анимации.

Motion specСпецификация движения
  • Debounce bursts.Гасите всплески. Coalesce updates within ~600ms so five people arriving reads as one soft swell, not five jumps.Объединяйте изменения за ~600мс, чтобы пятеро вошедших читались одной волной.
  • Cap the stack.Ограничьте стопку. Render max 4 circles + a “+” more-chip; the count text is the source of truth.Не более 4 кружков + «+»; число — источник истины.
