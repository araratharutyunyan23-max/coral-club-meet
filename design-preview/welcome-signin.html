Custom brandedTradeoff
Sign in with GoogleВойти через Google

A teal CTA in our own button language. Prettier & on-brand — but see the tradeoff below.

Recommendation — keep the official button. The Google SDK renders its own widget: we can set theme (outline / filled), shape (pill), size (~40 px) and the “signin_with” text — nothing else. Logo, wording and internal spacing are fixed. It carries built-in localization (the RU toggle shows Google's own “Войти через Google”), accessibility and the trust users expect from “Sign in with Google”.
The custom-button tradeoff. A branded teal button (right) matches the brand better, but it cannot use Google's One Tap / rendered flow — we'd wire OAuth manually, own the localization & a11y ourselves, and it may fall outside Google's branding guidelines. My call: ship the official button; spend the brand energy on the Signal field around it, not on the button itself.
